MA13

London College of Fashion’s Graduate School will be showcasing the work of more than 200 students throughout a festival of events running during December 2012 and February 2013, covering the subject areas of design, media and management, including work from all its postgraduate courses.

Catwalk

By invitation only

  • 15.02.13

Venue: Royal Opera House, London, WC2

London College of Fashion will be showing selected collections from MA Fashion Design Technology, MA Fashion Artefact and MA Fashion Footwear

Alumni Event

This is a ticketed event.

  • 18.02.13
  • 18.00 - 20.00

London College of Fashion, together with the University of the Arts London Alumni Association will be hosting an exclusive alumni drinks reception at the LCF 2013 Exhibition. The event, which will celebrate this year’s talented graduates, is a great networking opportunity and will raise money for the Student Support Campaign for LCF.

Please save the date and further details will be available soon.

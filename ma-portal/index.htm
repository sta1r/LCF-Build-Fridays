MA13

London College of Fashion’s Graduate School will be showcasing the work of more than 200 students throughout a festival of events running until February 2013, covering the subject areas of design, media and management, including work from all its postgraduate courses.

  • 1030 start

Venue: Royal Opera House, London, WC2

London College of Fashion will be showing selected collections from MA Fashion Design Technology, MA Fashion Artefact and MA Fashion Footwear

Media & Management

Two events showcasing the work of our postgraduate media and management students:

Fashion Management Breakfast
  • 14.02.13
  • 0900 for 0930, until 1130

Student presentations on Design Management, Strategic Fashion Marketing, Fashion Retail Management and Fashion Entrepreneurship

Pecha Kucha & Fashion and Film
  • 14.02.13
  • 1730 start

Film screening and graduate presentations on the History and Culture of Fashion, Fashion Journalism and Curation of Fashion

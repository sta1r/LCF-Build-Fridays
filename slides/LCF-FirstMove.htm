LCF Firstmove - The Future

Alastair Mucklow, London College of Fashion

LCF Firstmove

Plans for 2014

But what is it?

App

  • my.lcffirstmove.co.uk/login
  • Admins (you guys) log in to access student accounts, view and edit jobs and placements, and send emails
  • Students log in to apply for jobs, update their profiles
  • Recruiters log in to post opportunities

Wordpress

  • www.lcffirstmove.co.uk/wp-login.php
  • Publicly-viewable pages
  • Currently: competitions, profiles, resources

Problems

  • Island syndrome
  • Student confusion
  • Recruiter confusion?
  • Internal staff confusion?
  • App <> Wordpress performance issues

Solution

  • Remove Wordpress
  • Migrate content
    • Student resources, presentations, booking forms -> Moodle
    • Public content -> arts.ac.uk
  • Reskin the app
  • Relocate to lcffirstmove.arts.ac.uk
  • Access via arts.ac.uk/fashion/about/lcf-careers

Risks

  • Requires support and approval from UAL Digital Team and Central IT
  • Cost
  • Finding the right developer
